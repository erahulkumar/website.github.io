About Digital Affiliate


Digital Aff refers to marketing media that is digitally displayed. Digital Aff technology exists on the Internet, on smart phone and hand-held media devices, and even on automobiles and billboards. Businesses and product manufacturers use digital advertising to build or maintain a brand image and market products and services to consumers.






Audio Marketing



Audio advertising is a form of digital advertising that targets consumers through Podcasts, Internet radio and live streaming digital radio. Podcasts are digital audio files such as an MP3 that users download to a computer or personal media player.









Affiliate Marketing



Affiliate marketing is a type of performance-basedmarketing in which a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer brought by theaffiliate’s own marketing efforts.
